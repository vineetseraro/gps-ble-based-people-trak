Privacy Policy

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

The Application obtains the information you provide when you download and log in to the Application.

When you login with us and use the Application, you generally provide your name, email address, contact phone number, country and password.

Automatically Collected Information

Our apps will collect information from an end user's device any time the end user's app comes within range of a Stryker Authorized sensors Stryker Traquer relies on an app-specific identifier that is used to identify a unique app user. This identifier is associated only with the specific Traquer app and is not tied to your mobile device. If you delete the Traquer app from your device, this identifier will be deleted as well. Relying on this app-specific ID, we collect your location (latitude/longitude coordinates) and the time of day and your location when your device is near any of our sensors. We also collect device information, such as the device type, model and operating system version, and current IP address. We use this information to identify when you enter or leave the range of a particular sensor in a location.

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

When you are near any Stryker authorized sensors, we use GPS technology, wifi triangulation (and / or other similar technology) to determine your current location. This information is anonymously send to the server which only reports the location and the sensor which was recognized by the app.

The mobile app does not record your location when you are not in range of any Stryker authorized sensor.

We also create a log file in your device which we can ask you to send to us, for troubleshooting purposes.
